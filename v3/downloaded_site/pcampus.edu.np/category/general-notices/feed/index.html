Pulchowk Campus, Entrepreneurship Support Program is initiating a unique Training Course on Entrepreneurship. This course conduction will be different than other academic courses at least in three aspects; the mode of conduction will be on intensive workshop sessions with full of activities, the most of interactions and mentoring will be from entrepreneurs and related field experts, and the most important you will be investing your time only on the activities related to your passion.

Who knows your idea might be another Nepali Unicorn like ByteDance from China and FlipKart of India, as your idea might be one of the best ideas which could get seed fund ranging between 50K to 40 Lakh NRs!!!

In order to manage various associated logistics of this initiative, you are requested to fill-up a survey form, if you are craving for Start-up sooner or later.

An email is sent in your campus email address with the link of the form to be filled.
