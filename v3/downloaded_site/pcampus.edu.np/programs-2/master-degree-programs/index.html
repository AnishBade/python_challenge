Intake Capacities in Masters Programs

The quota distribution for all M.Sc. Programs on the current intake capacity of each program is listed as follows.

Masters Program Quota Regular  IOE Reserved Full Fee Sponsored Total Intake
Capacity 5 1 6 8 20
  1. One seat in the regular category and One seat in the full fee category are reserved for female candidates in each program
  2. One seat is reserved for IOE faculty. For IOE Reserved Quota, priority will be given to permanent IOE faculty. If there are no applicants from permanent IOE faculty, then priority will be given to faculties who have completed a minimum of 3 years as contract faculty. If there is no applicant in the IOE reserve quota, it is added to the Full Fee Seat. 1/4th of the sponsored quota is reserved for the foreign students in each program.
  3. Priority for the sponsored quota will be as follows:
    • First Priority: Candidates from government offices & Government Owned Organizations or Companies
    • Second Priority: Other Organizations
  4. If applications are not received in sponsored/foreign category, then the seat will be fulfilled as the Full Fee category.
  5. In any program, if 60% of the full fee and sponsored seats are not fulfilled within the first phase (first, second and third admission list) of the admission, that program will be suspended for that academic year.
